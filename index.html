What is Trivox

Trivox helps you turn raw ideas into structured workflows — using Python, AI, and clean logic. It's designed for developers who prefer full control and want to evolve their workflows over time.

What You Can Build

  • Automate creative content pipelines
  • Convert brainstorms into scoped projects
  • Run prompt-chained AI tasks
  • Create structured versions of creative ideas

Why It’s Different

  • Dev-first (not no-code)
  • AI where it helps — not where it controls
  • Modular, versioned, local-friendly
  • Terminal-first, but extendable to GUI
